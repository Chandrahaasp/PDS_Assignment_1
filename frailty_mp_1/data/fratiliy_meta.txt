Dataset: Frailty Study Dataset
Source: Hypothetical Study - Female Participants
Description: This dataset contains height, weight, age, grip strength, and frailty indicators.
Columns:
- Height: Participant height (Float, Inches)
- Weight: Participant weight (Float, Pounds)
- Age: Participant age (Integer, Years)
- Grip strength: Hand grip strength (Float, Kilograms)
- Frailty: Frailty status (String, Y=Yes, N=No)
